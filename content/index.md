# Welcome to the Derby Owners Club Wiki

The definitive, community-maintained resource for everything related to the Sega *Derby Owners Club* arcade experience. 

## Getting Started
* **[Contribute]**: We are actively looking for data on hardware and mechanics. Read our [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md).
* **[Browse Data]**: View our full [Taxonomy](TAXONOMY.md) to see what needs documentation.

## Featured Content
* **Hardware**: Technical manuals and maintenance for Sega cabinets (Nanao/Naomi).
* **Mechanics**: Deep dives into breeding strategies and race mechanics.
* **Events**: Coverage of community tournaments like the Breeder's Cups at Four Swords Retropub.

## Current Goals
* Documenting the full *Derby Owners Club* machine specifications.
* Standardizing data entry.
* Documenting gameplay and mechanics.
